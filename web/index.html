OUR GOAL


Global hunger isn't about a lack of food.
Right now, the world produces enough food to nourish every child, woman and man on the planet. But nearly a third of all food produced each year is squandered or lost before it can be consumed.
In many rich countries, this food waste happens in the kitchen — when we prepare foods that go uneaten, or leave food to spoil in fridges and kitchen cabinets.

Our website lets you rescue delicious, unsold food from businesses to save it from being thrown away and make sure it ends up at the right place. With the help of non profit organizations and businesses willing to provide, we are moving towards a 'no food wastage' future.

ABOUT US


We make sure good food gets eaten, not wasted.
Every day, delicious, fresh food goes to waste at cafés, restaurants, hotels, shops and manufacturers - just because it hasn’t sold in time.
Food for all connects various businesses and non profit organizations to establish communication and save food from going waste.
Businesses can register themselves and put up a notice when excess food is left over. Non profit organizations which are registered with us can ping the businesses and retrieve the food and feed the needy.
